The players take chance in rolling the dice.
The current score is intialised with 0 and gets updated on each roll of the dice.
However, when the dice rolls to 1 , the current score is reverted to zero.
The current score will be updated to the player score on each turn.
The player can hold the current value and pass the chance to the next player.
The player who first obtains the score >=100 wins the game.
